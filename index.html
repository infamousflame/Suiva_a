Our Product

The H2O Flow is a self regulating, moisture smart, water system that will help you save big time. H2O Flow uses sensors to detect the water content in the yard. The data collected from the sensors is will be detected by a control box. The control box then turns on only the sprinklers in the areas that need to be watered.

How it Works

H20 Flow is a smart sprinkler system. It measures the moisture content around individual sprinklers, and decides which ones to turn on to maximize water efficiency. We read the moisture content of the soil by inserting moisture sensors under the ground to read the live moisture content. The control board then will determine the moisture content of each individual sprinkler, then will decide whether or not to turn off each sprinkler with a wirelessly controlled water valve located in the sprinkler head.
